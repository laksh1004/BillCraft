## Full Description *(4 000 character budget)*

---

Are you still writing invoices by hand or struggling with expensive billing software? **BillCraft** is the fastest, simplest, and most powerful GST billing app built for Indian small businesses, shops, freelancers, and traders. Create professional tax invoices in under 30 seconds — no internet required.

Whether you run a kirana store in Mumbai, a tailoring shop in Jaipur, a plumbing business in Bengaluru, or a consulting firm in Delhi — BillCraft is designed for YOU.

---

### ✅ KEY FEATURES

**📋 GST-Ready Invoices**
Create fully compliant GST tax invoices with automatic CGST, SGST, and IGST calculations. Choose the correct tax type based on inter-state or intra-state supply — BillCraft handles the maths for you.

**🔢 GSTIN Validation**
Enter your GSTIN and your customer's GSTIN. BillCraft validates them instantly using the standard format check, so you never send an invoice with a wrong tax number again.

**📤 Share via WhatsApp, Email & PDF**
Send invoices directly to your customers over WhatsApp in one tap. Share as a professional PDF via email, or download it to your device. No extra steps, no printing required.

**👤 Customer Management**
Maintain a complete directory of your customers — name, phone, email, address, state, PIN code, and GSTIN. Link customers to invoices and track their outstanding balances at a glance.

**💰 Payment Tracking**
Record payments in Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, Cheque, or Card. BillCraft tracks partial payments and shows you the exact balance due on every invoice. Never miss a payment again.

**📲 UPI Payment QR Code on Invoices**
Add your UPI ID once in settings. BillCraft automatically prints a scannable UPI QR code on every invoice so your customers can pay you instantly without asking for your bank details.

**📑 Estimates & Quotations**
Send professional estimates to your clients before the work begins. Convert approved estimates to invoices in one tap — no re-entering data.

**🧾 Payment Receipts**
Generate clean payment receipts for your customers when they pay. Perfect for retail shops, contractors, and service providers.

**🏢 Business Profile with Bank Details**
Set up your business once — name, GSTIN, address, bank account, IFSC, UPI ID, and logo. All details appear automatically on every document you create.

**🔤 Amount in Words (Indian Format)**
Every invoice displays the grand total in words — "Rupees Fifteen Thousand Five Hundred Only" — in proper Indian numbering format, as required by GST law.

**🏷️ HSN / SAC Code Support**
Add HSN codes for goods and SAC codes for services on every line item. Essential for GST-compliant B2B invoices.

**📉 TDS Calculation**
Add TDS deductions (with section reference) directly on invoices. Useful for freelancers, consultants, and contractors dealing with TDS-deducting clients.

**📴 Works 100% Offline**
No internet? No problem. BillCraft stores everything securely on your device. Create invoices, record payments, and manage customers — all without a data connection. Internet is only needed for cloud backup (coming soon).

**🔒 Secure Local Storage**
Your business data never leaves your device without your permission. There are no mandatory sign-ups, no cloud subscriptions, and no data sold to third parties.

**📄 PDF Generation**
Generate pixel-perfect, print-ready PDF invoices that look professional on any device or printer. Share or archive as needed.
